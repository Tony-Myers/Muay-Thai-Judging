Core Principles

Top-Down Judging

We do not score round-by-round strikes. We judge the narrative. A boxer can score while moving backwards if they maintain balance and control.

Effect Over Volume

"Touching is not scoring." Points are awarded for visible impact, off-balancing, and damage that changes behavior.
