Muay Thai judging: effect, balance, and control over the whole fight

Muay Thai is judged by comparing who is stronger in Muay Thai terms over five rounds: visible effectiveness, dominance, balance/posture, and control. [oai_citation:3‡Guild of Muay Thai Officials judging presentation 2025.pdf](sediment://file_00000000054c71f4bdad69e3e27cbd60)

Key principle:

Scoring is not direction-dependent. You can score moving forward, backward, sideways, or on the ropes. Judges reward effective scoring with balance and control, not simply advancing.
