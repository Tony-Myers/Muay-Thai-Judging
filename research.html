Research

Research and publications

This page will host research articles, plain-language summaries, and podcast episodes relating to Muay Thai performance, judging, and analysis.

Research articles

Upload your PDFs to the repository (for example assets/downloads/) and link them here. Where possible, include the journal citation and a short “why it matters” summary.

  • Example entry: Article title (Year) — one sentence on the key finding.
  • Suggested format: Citation, downloadable PDF, 150–250 word summary, and a “Key takeaways” list.

Plain-language posts

Short posts for fighters and coaches: what was studied, what was found, and how (or whether) it should change practice.

  • Keep it tight: one screen of text, a diagram or clip if needed, and practical takeaways.
  • Be cautious: distinguish results from opinion and avoid over-claiming.

Podcasts and video discussions

Embed YouTube-hosted discussions that explain the research to a general audience.

Tip: use an accessible title, provide a short written summary for viewers who cannot play video, and include timestamps for key sections.
